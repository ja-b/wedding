Around Tokyo & Karuizawa

Where to eat & drink

A running list of our favourite spots for meals, coffee and drinks in both places, the ones we'd send our own friends to. We'll keep adding as the weekend gets closer.

In & around the city

Tokyo

Our marriage sponsor's bar · near the shrine

Bar After the Sunset

Just around the corner from Nogi-jinja in Akasaka, this is where we expect to land after the shrine ceremony on the 12th. Come raise a glass with us.

View on the map ↗

  • Kikunoi (菊乃井)Akasaka · Two-Michelin-star kaiseki, and where Ayumi works. The Tokyo home of the celebrated Kyoto restaurant Kikunoi, a short way from the shrine. Book well ahead.
  • SuzunariYotsuya (Arakicho) · A refined Japanese restaurant. Book ahead.
  • ConcertoYoyogi-Uehara · An Italian restaurant in our home neighborhood, with a good wine list.
  • Tonkatsu TakeshinYoyogi-Uehara · Excellent tonkatsu, and a real local favourite.
  • Sakutami (さくたみ)Shin-Koenji · Good Japanese food with live jazz, a fun evening out. Worth booking ahead.
  • Soba YamatoYoyogi-Uehara · A soba restaurant.
  • BAR MALShinjuku · Another old friend's bar. Go say hello.
  • Bar Old PalShibuya (Dōgenzaka) · A snug, classic cocktail bar.
  • The FamilyMart barShibuya · A proper bar hidden inside a FamilyMart convenience store. Only in Tokyo.
  • Hanaoka StoreWest Tokyo · A personal favourite of ours, a proper local spot, though a fair trek out from the centre.

Up in the mountains

Karuizawa

  • SawamuraOld Karuizawa · A well-known bakery and restaurant, good for breakfast or lunch.
  • GonseOld Karuizawa · A smart izakaya on the old main street, with a good sake and wine list.
  • La LuceNaka-Karuizawa · A local Italian restaurant.
  • Gelateria GinaA good gelato shop in Karuizawa.
  • Piggies CafeKita-Karuizawa · A casual cafe, if you head up to Kita-Karuizawa.
  • Cocktail Works KaruizawaOld Karuizawa · A cocktail bar known for craft gin and seasonal fruit cocktails.

The venue's own restaurant, SONORITÉ, is well regarded too, serving French cooking with local ingredients, right at Kyukaruizawa Kikyo.
